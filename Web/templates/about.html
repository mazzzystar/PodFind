PodFind.xyz

1. Initial intention

PodFind.xyz is not designed to be a podcast search engine.
In fact, I would prefer it to be helping you gain new awareness. You can:
  • Search for any keywords to know what Podcasters thoughts on a person, or an event.
  • Click on timestamps get a bird's-eye view of how KOLs view and judge new events, such as Musk acquiring Twitter, Silicon Valley Bank, GPT-4, and more.

2. Why choose podcasts

In terms of format, podcasts require recording equipment and multi-person conversations, which act as a filter and also provide possibilities for in-depth viewpoint exchanges. Therefore, I tend to believe that the signal-to-noise ratio(SNR) of podcasts is higher.

3. Free to use?

Yes! It's purely for fun. If server costs increase, I'll accept donations to keep it alive longer.

4. Future plans

This version is too basic. Many audio files don't have timestamps. In the future, I'll add Whisper to build a timeline for each episode, so you can search for any keywords and directly listen to relevant segments.

Anyway, have fun!
